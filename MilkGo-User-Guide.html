CHAPTER ONEWhy MilkGo · Problems & Solutions

"Running a dairy business on a paper khata is hard."
Here are the everyday problems milkmen, mandalis, tabela owners and shop owners face — and how MilkGo fixes each one.

📒 Problem 1 — Khata pages get lost, smudged, torn

Before: Pencil entries in a register. By month‑end, you can't read who got how much milk on the 14th.

After: Every delivery is one tap on your phone. Saved instantly. Searchable forever.

📊 Problem 2 — Monthly billing takes 2–3 days

Before: Sit at the table on the 1st of every month, add up 30 days × multiple products × 100+ customers + previous balance.

After: Tap "Generate All Bills". Done in seconds. Bills are also auto‑updated as you mark each delivery.

🤝 Problem 3 — Customer disputes

Before: "I never got 1 L on the 14th." You have no proof. You either lose money or lose trust.

After: Every delivery sends a WhatsApp update to the customer the moment you mark it. They have proof. You have proof. No fight.

🌴 Problem 4 — Forgotten skips and pauses

Before: Customer told you on the phone "skip Sunday". You forgot. Delivered anyway. Now both are confused.

After: Set "skip" with one swipe, or pause the subscription. Auto‑remembered. Customer also gets a WhatsApp note.

💸 Problem 5 — No idea who owes how much

Before: Flip through 100 pages of khata to find pending dues. You always miss someone.

After: Customers tab shows pending balance next to every name, sorted by who owes most or who's overdue longest.

🚚 Problem 6 — Driver hand‑off mistakes

Before: You hand a paper list to your driver. They tick wrong rows. Cash collected doesn't match.

After: Driver gets only their own route on their phone. Marks delivered / skipped on each card. You see it live.

🛒 Problem 7 — Counter sales mixed with monthly customers

Before: Walk‑in cash gets written somewhere, monthly khata somewhere else. Totals don't tie.

After: Counter mode is a separate tab. Tap product → confirm → done. Today's counter sales total is always visible.

📱 Problem 8 — No proof for the customer

Before: Customer asks "did you deliver yesterday?" — you have nothing to show them.

After: Customer's phone has every delivery on WhatsApp. They can scroll back any day they want.

💱 Problem 9 — Rate changes are messy

Before: Cow milk goes from ₹60 to ₹65. Some entries still use ₹60. Disputes.

After: Change the rate on the product. New deliveries pick it up automatically. Past bills stay as they were.

📞 Problem 10 — Hours wasted chasing payment

Before: Call 50 customers every week — half don't pick up.

After: Tap "Send All Reminders" — WhatsApp goes to every unpaid customer with the bill PDF and a "View Bill" button.

🌅 Problem 11 — Multi‑shift / multi‑product confusion

Before: A family takes buffalo morning, cow evening, ghee weekly, paneer Sundays. You miss things.

After: One subscription per product. Frequencies (daily / alternate / weekly / custom). Morning, evening, or both. The app handles it.

📈 Problem 12 — No idea how the business is doing

Before: "Did I make more this month than last month?" Nobody knows.

After: Reports tab shows revenue, collection rate, top customers, top products, shift split — with charts.

CHAPTER FOURGetting Started

Sign up to first delivery in 30 minutes.

1️⃣ Open MilkGo

  • Web: https://milkgo.in on any phone or computer browser
  • Android app: search "MilkGo" on Play Store, install

2️⃣ Login

  1. Enter your 10‑digit mobile number
  2. Tap "Send OTP"
  3. Enter the 6‑digit OTP from your SMS
  4. New numbers get a short signup form. Existing users go to PIN setup.

3️⃣ Set your PIN

  • Create an 8‑digit PIN
  • Re‑enter to confirm
  • This is what you'll use to open the app daily — faster than OTP every time

4️⃣ Pick role and language

  • Tabela / Mandali / Shop / Milkman / Driver
  • ગુજરાતી / हिन्दी / English (changeable later in Settings)

5️⃣ Fill basic profile

  • Full Name
  • Business / Dairy Name
  • City and Area
  • Done — you're in 🎉

🆓 Free trial

🎁
You start with a free trial. The dashboard banner shows your trial end date. To continue beyond trial, contact +91 94266 98106 or support@milkgo.in.

✅ First‑day setup checklist

  1. Areas → add delivery localities (e.g. Vesu, Adajan, Piplod). Add the societies inside each area.
  2. Products → add products (e.g. Buffalo Milk ₹70/litre, Cow Milk ₹60/litre, Ghee, Paneer). Add Gujarati names too.
  3. Customers → add each customer with society + wing + flat. If they already owe you money on paper khata, fill "Opening Balance".
  4. Subscriptions → for each customer, set their daily milk (product + qty + shift + frequency).
  5. Deliveries → tap "Generate" on the Deliveries tab.
  6. Mark deliveries as you do your round.

That's it. Bills will start appearing automatically by month‑end. ✨

CHAPTER FIVEA Day in the Life

"What does a typical day look like?"
Here's a realistic walkthrough for a milkman with 100 customers.

🌅 5:30 AM — Open the app

  • Enter your 8‑digit PIN
  • Dashboard greets you: "Good morning, Ramesh"
  • Today's Deliveries card shows "0 / 102 done"

📋 5:35 AM — Generate today's list

  • Tap Deliveries at the bottom
  • Confirm date is today, shift is Morning
  • If list is empty, tap Generate → "102 deliveries generated"

🚶 5:45 AM — Start the round

  • Pick up the first society's milk crate
  • Open the app at the customer's door
  • Swipe right on their card → ✅ Done
  • 💬 Customer's WhatsApp pings instantly with the delivery receipt

🎯 Special cases on the round

  • Customer not home → swipe left → tap Skip → reason "Not Home" → save
  • Customer wants only 0.5 L instead of 1 L → tap Edit → change qty → status Partial → save
  • Multi‑product customer → swipe right on the grouped card → all items mark Done in one swipe → 💬 ONE combined WhatsApp message

🏁 8:30 AM — Round done

  • Open Deliveries → see Done 98 / Pending 0 / Skipped 4
  • Tap the donut icon → see product‑wise quantities for stock reconciliation

🛒 11:00 AM — Counter walk‑ins

  • Tap Counter → search customer → add products → Confirm Sale
  • Recent Sales list grows

🌆 5:30 PM — Evening round

  • Same as morning, but switch the shift toggle to Evening
  • Tap Generate if needed

🌙 9:00 PM — End of day

  • Tap Reports → today's sales, deliveries, payment methods
  • Quick check of overdue customers list on the dashboard
  • Lock app

No paperwork. No "let me write it down later". 😌

CHAPTER SIXMigrating from Paper Khata

You don't throw away the paper register on day one. You migrate cleanly.

📅 Step 0 — Pick a "switch‑over date"

Decide a date, say 1 May 2026. From that date, everything goes into MilkGo. Before that date, paper.

📝 Step 1 — One evening: enter customers

  • Open Customers → Add Customer
  • For each row in your paper khata: Name, phone, area, society, wing, flat
  • Opening Balance = whatever they owe you as of 30 April. Most important field — carries old balance forward.
  • WhatsApp opt‑in if they're OK with it
💡
If you have 200 customers, plan 2–3 hours total.

🥛 Step 2 — Add products

All your products with rates as of switch‑over date.

🔁 Step 3 — Add subscriptions

For each customer, what they take daily — frequency, shift, qty, rate.

🧪 Step 4 — Test with one day

  • On 1st May morning, Generate today's deliveries
  • Mark a few done as a test
  • Check the customer's WhatsApp message arrives correctly

📒 Step 5 — Run parallel for 2 weeks

  • Keep writing in paper too, just to be safe
  • Cross‑check daily totals
  • After 2 weeks of clean match → close the paper register

📤 Step 6 — Generate the first bill on 1 June

  • Tap Generate All Bills
  • Bills include opening balance + May's deliveries + May's counter sales
  • Send via WhatsApp

⚠️ Common migration mistakes

⚠️
Forgetting opening balances → customers won't see old dues. Fix: edit each customer, fill Opening Balance.
⚠️
Wrong rate → if you migrated mid rate change, double‑check.
⚠️
Subscription start date in the past → MilkGo tries to generate deliveries for past days. Use today as start date.

CHAPTER SEVENMonthly Closing Workflow

The 30 minutes that used to take 3 days.

📅 1st of the month — generate bills

  1. Open Billing / Khata
  2. Tap Generate All Bills
  3. The app creates one consolidated bill per customer with last month's deliveries + counter sales + previous unpaid balance
  4. Confirmation: "84 customers billed" 🎉

📤 Same day — send all bills

  1. In the Billing tab, tap Send All Due
  2. Filter: pick last month, set minimum due if you want to skip tiny amounts
  3. Preview customer list (ones without WhatsApp are flagged)
  4. Tap Send 84 Bills
  5. Watch live progress: 84/84 sent ✓
  6. Failed sends are listed separately so you can retry

🔔 7th — send reminders to non‑payers

  1. Billing → status filter "Generated" or "Partial"
  2. Tap Send All Reminders
  3. Each reminder includes a "View Bill" button
  4. Cooldown: same customer won't be reminded within 24 hours

💵 15th — collection day

  1. Reports → Customers → Top Overdue Customers
  2. Call them in order — biggest dues first
  3. Record each payment as it comes in (Quick Collect on the bill)
  4. 💬 Customer gets WhatsApp receipt automatically

📊 30th — review the month

  1. Reports → Overview → "Last Month"
  2. Check Collection Rate. If under 80%, tighten reminders next month.
  3. Check Top Customers — say thank you to your best ones
  4. Check Product Breakdown — which products grew, which shrank

That's your closing routine. Repeat every month. 🔁

CHAPTER NINECustomers

🎯 What it's for

Your master customer list. Every customer is here.

📍 Where to find it

Bottom nav → Customers (people icon).

➕ How to add a customer

  1. Tap Add Customer
  2. Enter Name, Phone, Area, Society, Wing/Floor/Flat, Default product/qty/shift, Notes
  3. Customer Type — Delivery, Counter, or Both
  4. WhatsApp section: toggle "Enable WhatsApp" → ON; WhatsApp Number (leave empty if same as phone)

Customers are never deleted — only archived. Tap trash → confirm. Restore by enabling "Show Inactive" → re‑activate.

📡 NFC link Premium

Customer detail → "Link NFC" → tap card to phone. At counter, tap card → customer auto‑selected.

CHAPTER TWELVESubscriptions

"This is the heart of MilkGo."
A recurring delivery agreement — "Patel family — 1 L buffalo milk every morning". Set once, runs daily.

Menu → Subscriptions.

CHAPTER THIRTEENDeliveries

The screen you'll use most.

🔁 Daily flow

  1. Pick the Date (defaults to today)
  2. Pick the Shift — Morning or Evening
  3. Tap Generate if list is empty
  4. See all customers grouped by Area → Society → Customer

🃏 Each delivery card shows

  • Customer code (C123) and name
  • Wing‑Flat (e.g. "A‑502")
  • Product name and quantity
  • Status icon: 🟡 pending · 🟢 delivered · 🔴 skipped · 🟠 partial
  • Quick action buttons (edit, skip)

👆 Swipe gestures

  • Swipe right → ✅ Done (or Undo if already done)
  • Swipe left → shows two buttons: ✏️ Edit and ⏭️ Skip (or Undo if already skipped)

📦 Multi‑product customers

If one customer takes 3 products in one shift, the app shows ONE grouped card with all 3 inside. Swipe right → marks ALL three Done in one go and sends one combined WhatsApp message.

✏️ Edit a delivery

Tap the pencil (or swipe left → Edit). Change Status, Actual quantity, Skip reason (Not Home / Gate Locked / Customer Request / Holiday / Rain / Other), Notes. Save → customer gets a WhatsApp update.

🔒 Locked deliveries

Already in a finalised bill → can't change directly (lock icon). Edit the bill first, or contact support.

📊 Summary cards (top)

  • Done count / Total
  • Pending count
  • Skipped count
  • Total Quantity in litres

💬 What goes to WhatsApp

  • Every Done / Skip → one WhatsApp message
  • Every Undo → "delivery update reverted"
  • Every Edit save → updated WhatsApp message
  • Bulk delivery on a multi‑product customer → ONE combined message

  5. Tap Confirm Sale — done. Bill auto‑updates.

Adds the same items as the customer's last purchase.

💬 WhatsApp toggle

A small switch above the cart — "Send WhatsApp update to customer". On by default if "Counter Update" pref is on.

💡 Tips

  • Use NFC for regulars — 5 seconds per sale
  • Counter sales DON'T affect the delivery list

  • PIN screen → "Forgot PIN? Verify with OTP"
  • Enter OTP from SMS → set new PIN

💵 "Bill amount looks wrong"

  • Open bill → check line items
  • Check previous balance — it carries forward
  • If a delivery is locked, delete bill and regenerate

🕐 "Counter sale showing on wrong day"

App uses Asia/Kolkata (IST). Sale at 11:55 PM on 25th shows in 25th's totals. Fix phone clock if wrong.

🚚 "Driver can't see deliveries"

  • Driver has a route assigned?
  • Route's "Days of Week" includes today?
  • Route's stops have customers?
  • Today's deliveries generated?

🚫 "Plan limit reached"

Tap banner → "View Plan" → contact support.

🗃️ "Deleted a customer by mistake"

Customers tab → "Show Inactive" → find them → re‑activate.

↩️ "Refund / reverse a payment"

Open payment → Delete → confirm. Bill auto‑reverses.

🎯 "Marked delivery for wrong customer"

Deliveries → find customer → swipe to Undo → mark correct customer.

📑 "Two bills for same customer same month"

Probably tapped Generate twice. Open one → delete it. Latest "generated_at" is the correct one.

📲 "WhatsApp says 'this number is not on WhatsApp'"

Check WhatsApp Number on customer profile. Try without country code (just 10 digits).

  • Added in Drivers tab, not Customers?
  • Use the phone number you entered when adding
  • First login is OTP, then they set PIN

📊 "Reports look weird"

Reports use IST. Fix phone clock if wrong.

🛒 "Can't see Counter tab"

Counter is for Tabela / Mandali / Shop / Super‑admin. Milkman role hides it.
